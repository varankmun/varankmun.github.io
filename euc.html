About the committee

The European Union Council epitomizes regional unity and cooperation. As it tackles the escalating refugee crisis, this issue has become a litmus test for European solidarity and its humanitarian values. Delegates will explore the necessary policies and frameworks to manage the increasing number of refugees, focusing on security, integration, and human rights. The discussions will directly influence the EU's strategies for asylum, fostering an inclusive environment, and upholding Europe's core values. Engage with the European Union Council as we navigate the complexities of this significant humanitarian challenge, aiming to develop compassionate and practical solutions.
