DISEC

About the committee

As the first line of defense against the proliferation of weapons of mass destruction, DISEC stands at the forefront of global security efforts. This discussion will focus on the critical and timely issue of preventing the use of chemical and biological weapons. These weapons pose a grave threat to humanity, capable of causing unprecedented devastation and suffering. Delegates will delve into the complexities of arms control, explore innovative verification mechanisms, and forge international cooperation to eliminate these threats. In this vital mission, delegates will work to develop robust frameworks and policies to prevent the deployment of these destructive weapons, ensuring a safer future for all.
