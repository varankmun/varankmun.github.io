Letter from Secretary General

Esteemed Delegates,

I am thrilled to welcome you to VarankMUN'24, a prestigious conference that promises to be a memorable experience for all participants. As the Secretary General, I am honored to guide you through this journey of diplomacy, debate, and collaboration.

VarankMUN'24 brings together bright minds from diverse backgrounds, all united by a passion for making a difference. Over these four days, you will have the opportunity to step into the shoes of diplomats, representing various countries and people while engaging in discussions on important global issues. Your unique perspectives and thoughtful contributions will be key to shaping meaningful resolutions and fostering mutual understanding.

At VarankMUN'24, we aim to create an environment where every delegate feels empowered to voice their opinions and engage with one another in a spirit of respect and open-mindedness. Our committees offer a platform for constructive dialogue, critical thinking, and creative problem- solving. You will not only gain valuable insights into the workings of international diplomacy but also develop skills that will serve you well beyond the conference.

Our Academic and Organization teams have worked tirelessly to organize a conference that is both enriching and enjoyable. From engaging committee sessions to networking opportunities, we hope that VarankMUN'24 will be an unforgettable experience for you. I want to express my gratitude for your participation and commitment to this conference. Your enthusiasm and dedication are what make VarankMUN'24 possible. I encourage you to make the most of this opportunity, embrace the challenges, and don’t forget to have fun with your friends. Thank you for being a part of VarankMUN'24.

I look forward to seeing the incredible impact you will make during the conference.

Best wishes,

Barbaros Şıracı

Secretary General
