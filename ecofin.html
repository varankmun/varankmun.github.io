ECOFIN

About the committee

ECOFIN is pivotal in shaping the financial stability and economic policies that drive global prosperity. In light of rapid technological advancements and recent global challenges, the council is set to tackle two critical issues: the complex taxation of the digital economy and the security of international supply chains. With digital transactions seamlessly crossing borders, traditional tax systems struggle to keep pace, necessitating innovative approaches for fair taxation. Simultaneously, the vulnerabilities of global supply chains, exposed by recent disruptions, require comprehensive strategies to maintain the steady flow of goods and services. Delegates will engage in rigorous discussions to craft solutions that address these modern economic challenges. Dive into ECOFIN to forge policies that harmonize the demands of innovation and regulation, and to build a robust framework for a resilient and equitable global economy.
