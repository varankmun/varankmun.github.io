About the committee

Step back in time with the Historical Joint Crisis Committee to the year 1177, where the fate of the Kingdom of Jerusalem hangs in the balance at the Battle of Montgisard. In this intense simulation, delegates will assume roles within the Christian and Muslim forces, navigating the intricate political and military strategies of the era. The Kingdom of Jerusalem, led by a young and determined Baldwin IV, faces the formidable forces of the Ayyubid Dynasty under Saladin. Participants will explore the dynamics of medieval warfare, diplomacy, and leadership in a scenario that could alter the course of history. Engage in the HJCC’s dramatic reenactment and test your prowess in a clash that echoes through the centuries.
